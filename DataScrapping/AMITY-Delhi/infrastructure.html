Q:   What is the hostel fees of Amity University, Noida? Are there facilities like gym?

A:

The hostel fees at Amity University, Noida vary depending on the type of room and the facilities included. For example, a single AC room with attached bathroom costs approximately INR 1,60,000 per year, while a triple non-AC room with common bathroom costs around INR 85,000 per year. The hostel fees include meals, laundry, and security. However, students will need to pay for their own books, stationery, and other personal expenses. Yes, the hostel at Amity University, Noida has a gym. The gym is equipped with a variety of equipment, including treadmills, ellipticals, weight machines, and free weights. The gym is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. In addition to the gym, the hostel also has a number of other facilities, including: 

  • A canteen
  • A laundry room
  • A common room
  • A library
  • A medical center
  • A security room

The hostel is located on the university campus, which means that students have easy access to all of the university's facilities, such as the library, the sports complex, and the academic buildings. Overall, the hostel at Amity University, Noida is a good option for students who want to live on campus. The hostel is clean, safe, and well-maintained. It also has a number of facilities that students will need, such as a gym, a canteen, and a laundry room.
